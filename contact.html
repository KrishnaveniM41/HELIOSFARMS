HELIOS RESORTS

Nature is the best artist, and this property is a masterpiece of its creation. Whether you are a bachelor or a family, we have a 2- bedroom villa reserved for you. This holiday home is cradled 3000 feet above sea level in Uluppuni meadows, just 120 KM from Vagamon town. Here you can enjoy the scenic beauty of mountains and valley views—the ultimate place for a serene vacation. And here you will experience the magic of the movie "Iyobinte Pusthakam", which was shot at this location. You can enjoy those enchanting views from your balcony or step outdoors on the endless grassland. If you are looking for a cosy and comfortable place to relax and rejuvenate, this is the place where you should be heading next. This is ideal for travellers looking for a peaceful mountain holiday destination.

HELIOS FARMS

With an unending view of the lush green Vagamon meadows, it's like being in a dream amidst nature. You will step into the courtyard, and the stunning view of the majestic mountains among the floating clouds will mesmerise you. The villa comprises 2 rooms, each with private balconies overlooking the mountain slope. The amazing views are yours to savour in the comforts of your luxurious suite or courtyard.
